Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by Chef Michael Haus, Ultimate Gourmet Haus started as a small food truck with a big dream — to serve the most delicious, high-quality food in town. What began as a weekend hobby quickly turned into a local sensation.

Our commitment to using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and crafting each dish with care helped us grow from that single food truck to our current brick-and-mortar location.

Our Philosophy

At Ultimate Gourmet Haus, we believe in:

  • Using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients
  • Offering a diverse menu that goes beyond just burgers, including pizzas, pastas, and salads
  • Serving food made with care, passion, and attention to detail
  • Creating unique, flavor-packed dishes for every palate
  • Providing a comfortable, welcoming environment for all guests

Our chefs use a blend of traditional and innovative cooking techniques to create unforgettable meals. We offer a variety of cuisines — from hearty burgers and gourmet pizzas to fresh, crisp salads and mouthwatering pastas. Whether you're in the mood for something savory or sweet, we have something for everyone. Our current chef is Gordon James Ramsay.
